Features

brickchat offers the highest user experience

Via the chat interaction, the user is led through a conversation and never loses the overview

  • Simple chat experience with speed letter answer option
  • Clear flow with simple interaction - complex GUIs aren't needed anymore
  • History of interactions can be viewed and adjusted

brickchat is easy

  • brickchat consists of a microservice ("nodes") silo that can be adapted on the fly
  • brickchat developers get access to a chat builder that allowes quick customizations of the micro.

brickchat is extremely scalable

  • The simple data model in the form of content, options and nodes makes it easy to add additional data and allow millions of interaction points
  • Smart background algorithms link already existing nodes and generic content. So, no need to start from scratch. “bricks” such as communications about pizza ordering processes already exist and are adjustable

brickchat is integrable

  • brickchat is event-based: all incoming messages are consumed by subscribing microservices that can reach out to outside services such as whatsapp, Zapier, Google APIs, Telegram, etc. etc.
  • Through its decentralized structure, services can either be deployed within brickchat’s infrastructure or on third-party servers

How it works

brickchat is doing the heavylifting of chatbots

the brickchat framework consists of content, option and services that provide dynamic data, such as available stocks, weather data or any other data from outside brickchat servers. These services are lean scripts triggered by callback/webhook methodology.
